Split-It

Split It is a web app that I helped create during my time at Le Wagon Tokyo. My fellow group members and I coded the entire project over a period of 2 weeks in June 2021, and I pitched it at Le Wagon's Pitch Night on Friday, June 11 to a crowd of ~80 on Zoom. My main contribution was on the front-end, although I had a hand in almost area.

We began with an almost day-long brainstorm session, where we figured out the core user journey. We decided that the app was aimed at people in relationships who have separate bank accounts and need to split shared expenses evenly.

Chef Space

Chef Space is an AirBnb clone website that I helped create during my time studying at Le Wagon in Tokyo. My fellow group members and I coded the entire project over a period of 5 days in May 2021. For this project, I was the project manager and also contributed to all parts of the project.

We decided on a website where chefs who needed restaurant space could rent spaces from those that owned them. We made extensive use of Mapbox to pinpoint the restaurant locations on an interactive map.
